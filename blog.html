Master's to PhD

August 10, 2020 /

It just happened!
It was a very unprecedented time when there was a high possibility that all companies might have to go remote for a few more months. There was a hiring freeze in most companies and lay-offs in others. Tough time for all students. I always wanted to do PhD so I took my chance and asked my advisor about the possibility of me coming back to the university maybe after two years of experience for a PhD degree. He advised me to apply for PhD right away if I wish to do one. He advised me if I wanted to really do PhD thenn why not do it right away and continue my studies.
After thinnking for sometime, I decided that given the current situation in the job market, I might as well take up PhD right now.
And so it just happened because I was in the right place at the right time!
